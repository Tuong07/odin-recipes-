One pan lemon herb chicken and viggies

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup baby carrots
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instruction

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the halved baby potatoes, baby carrots, and broccoli florets.
  3. Add minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Toss to coat the vegetables evenly.
  4. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  5. In a large oven-safe skillet or baking dish, place the seasoned chicken breasts in the center.
  6. Arrange the seasoned vegetables around the chicken in the skillet or dish.
  7. Lay lemon slices on top of each chicken breast.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  9. Serve the chicken and veggies together on a plate.
