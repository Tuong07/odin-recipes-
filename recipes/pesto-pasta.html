Pesto pasta

Ingredients :

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts or walnuts
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions :

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, grated Parmesan, garlic, and pine nuts or walnuts.
  3. Pulse the mixture while gradually adding the olive oil until you get a smooth pesto sauce.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce, adding a little reserved pasta water if needed to coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with additional grated Parmesan and some fresh basil leaves before serving.
